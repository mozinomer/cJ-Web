Trusted Jeweller since 1955, we are proud to be one of the first family-owned independent jewellery stores in Calgary. We are renowned for one of the city’s largest collection of brand name watches, jewellery, bridal bands, engagement rings, diamonds; and exceptional in-house designs by Bernard Florence. We also boast an impressive collection of writing instruments, crystal and giftware with prominent brands such as MontBlanc, Lalique, Baccarat and Daum.
